Display tweets from your contacts and companies in any widget area of RoloPress.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the twitter-tweets-for-rolopress folder to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Add the widget to your sidebar from Appearance->Widgets and configure the widget options.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can multiple instances of the widget be used? =

Yes.

= Can private Twitter accounts be used? =

No.

= Why are the tweets not showing? =

The widget will only display tweets on the Contact or Company Single pages.
